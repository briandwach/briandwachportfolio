About

Brian Wach is an audio-visual technician, musician, and composer in the Chicagoland area. He completed his master’s degree in Recording Arts and earned a bachelor’s in Percussion Performance, both at Northern Illinois University (NIU). Brian strives to combine his passion for technology and music in order to create inspiring and meaningful experiences for others. Since 2014, he has been working in event production and information technologies as a graduate assistant at NIU, audio warehouse manager with Sound Investment, and freelance streaming technician/virtual meeting producer for Brella Productions and Intempio. Before the current health pandemic, Brian could also be found playing/recording music with his acoustic duo, Off the Curb, sound engineering for the Emanation Music Collective, and performing with the improv comedy team, All Caps.
